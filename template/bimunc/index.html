What
is BIMUN?

Bhubaneswar International Model United Nations (BIMUN) is a simulation of an actual United Nations conference, where participants are tasked with solving a global issue through research, drafting, lobbying and debate to pass a suitable ‘resolution’. They take on the roles of delegates and chairs, representing different countries and work together with other participants from different backgrounds to take part in discussions and reach a solution for the agenda they have been assigned.

THE GLOBAL GOALS FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

BIMUN have increasingly envisioned to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals and facilitation of youths in the very same, besides skill building of students and exploring innovative practices and models of development for educational institutions towards an increased impact in the local community.
