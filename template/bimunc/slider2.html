Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Bhubaneswar International Model UN conference began with the vision to promote understanding of international affairs, sustainability and awareness of the United Nations among youths. By engaging in topics concerning about security, human rights, economic development and social progress, delegates learn to navigate the complexities of international negotiation and teamwork, adopt different perspectives and develop comprehensive resolutions to tackle pressing global issues.

BIMUN have increasingly envisioned to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals and facilitation of youths in the very same, besides skill building of students and exploring innovative practices and models of development for educational institutions towards an increased impact in the local community. Thus BIMUN always provides a platform for gaining unprecedented knowledge and a way to broadcast them.

BIMUN

Our Mission

Bhubaneswar International Model United Nations advances in the proper understanding of the United Nations and achievement of the 17 global goals. Our aim is to holistically impact on delegates which shall inspire them to take proper action over major global issues. Enlightening them to raise a voice in order to bring in the change which is required. Thus, bringing them together to formulate ideas in order for a better future.
