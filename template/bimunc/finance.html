Financial Aid & Guide to Fund raising

There is no financial aid available to support applicants at this time. We suggest applicants to check local organizations for sources of subsidy and support. Delegates can apply sponsorship to certain company/organization or school/college. Delegates may use the proposal and letter of acceptance as necessary document for sponsorship approval.

The steps : After you are registered and accepted as Delegate in BIMUN you will receive your Letter of Acceptance & Confirmation emails You can use your LOA as the attached page on the Sponsorship Proposal. You can submit your sponsorship Proposal to your school or university, alumnae group, or lecturer funding, or even local government.
